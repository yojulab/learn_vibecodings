## 2. Key Components & Relationships

- **CoCoLabHub (Core Concept)**
  - **Offers:** AI Frontier, Main Services
  - **Goal:** Innovation in Collaboration, Coding, and Language.

- **AI Frontier (Feature)**
  - **Provides:** Innovative AI Solutions
  - **For:** Developers and Educators

- **Main Services (Offerings)**
  - **Includes:**
    1.  **CodeInDocker:** Focuses on source code sharing for developers.
    2.  **LecturePlans:** Focuses on preparing lecture materials with AI.

- **User Actions (Calls to Action)**
  - Learn More (AI Frontier)
  - Visit Service (CodeInDocker, LecturePlans)
  - About / Main Services (Header/Hero)
